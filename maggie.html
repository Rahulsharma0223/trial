steps to make maggie

  1. Take 2 tea cup water for a packet of maggie and boil it.
  2. Add maggie masala once the water starts boiling.
  3. After 3 mins add the crush maggie in water and let it cook on medium gas.
  4. After 3 mins put the gas low and continuesly stear the maggie.
  5. Once you get odour of maggie , it is ready to be served.
  6. Serve it into bowl and enjoy your maggie.
